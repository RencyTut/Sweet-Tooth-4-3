Egg Pie

If you’ve ever had a custard pie before then you already know how good this Filipino dessert is.Egg Pie is made with eggs, sugar, flour, and milk and it’s baked in a pie crust. It can be served hot or cold and it’s always a hit when you have it at a party or gathering. It’s an easy recipe to follow and the end result is always delicious. You can also add in your favorite flavors to give it a little bit of a twist. This is definitely a dessert that you’ll want to add to your regular rotation because everyone will love it.

instructions

step 1

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Roll out the pie crust and fit it into a 9-inch pie dish. Trim the edges and prick the bottom of the crust with a fork. Set aside.

step 2

In a mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, evaporated milk, condensed milk, vanilla extract, and salt until well combined.

step 3

Add the water to the egg mixture and whisk it in to thin out the mixture.

step 4

Pour the egg mixture into the prepared pie crust.

step 5

Bake the egg pie for 45-50 minutes, or until the filling is set and the top is golden brown.

step 6

Remove the egg pie from the oven and let it cool to room temperature.

step 7

Slice the egg pie into wedges and serve it as a delicious dessert.

ingredients

1 (9-inch), store-bought or homemade Pie crust

4 large Eggs

1 can (12 ounces) Evaporated milk

1 can (14 ounces) Condensed milk

1 teaspoon Vanilla extract

1/4 teaspoon Salt

1/2 cup Water

Nutrition

Calories 200-350 KCal
Fat 10-15g
Carbohydrates 10-15g
Fiber 0-1g
Sugars 2-6g
Protein 10-20g
