Cebuana Torta Cake

Cebuana Torta is a Visayan sponge cake popularly served for dessert or as a midday snack. Itâ€™s delicious and simple to make with easy-to-find ingredients. Bake it at home using this easy recipe with step-by-step photos and tips!

Prep Time

15 min.

Cook Time

30 min.

Servings

12 servings

instructions

step 1

Preheat the oven to 350 F. Grease the insides of the mammon or brioche molds. Set aside.

step 2

In a large bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt until well combined. Set aside.

step 3

In a large bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt until well combined. Set aside.

step 4

Add the egg yolks and whisk until well-incorporated.

step 5

Add the milk and stir well.

step 6

Gradually add the flour mixture to the liquid mixture, stirring until the mixture is smooth.

step 7

Add the vanilla extract and stir.

step 8

Ladle the batter into the prepared molds until 3/4 full.

step 9

Arrange the filled molds on a baking tray and bake at 350 F for 25 to 30 minutes, or until the tops are lightly golden.

step 10

Remove the torta from heat and let cool slightly.

step 11

Brush the tops with softened butter or margarine and sprinkle with sugar.

Additional Note

Do not overwork the batter. Beat the mixture just until smooth and free of any streak of flour. The batter should be smooth and flowy but is not too runny.

The molds used in the recipe are 3.5 inches in diameter and 1 inch deep. If using a larger size, bake the torta for another 5 to 8 minutes.

ingredients

1 3/4 cup all-purpose flour

1 tablespoon baking powder

1/2 teaspoon salt

8 egg yolks (from large eggs), at room temperature

1 1/2 cups sugar

3/4 cup canola oil

1/4 cup butter, melted

1 cup evaporated milk, room temperature

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Margarine or butter for brushing on the torta

Sugar, for sprinkling on top
