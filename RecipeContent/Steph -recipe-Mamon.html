Mamon

Mamon is a type of sponge cake that is popular in the Philippines and other parts of the world. It is made with flour, sugar, eggs, and butter, and may include other ingredients like milk or baking powder. It can be served with a variety of different toppings but they’re usually dusted with sugar or cinnamon. Mamon is a popular choice for gatherings and special occasions and it’s always a welcome dessert choice. These are the perfect substitution if you’re looking for a new recipe to use for a birthday cake because you’ll get the same texture but with a wonderful Filipino taste.

instructions

step 1

Preheat your oven to 325°F (160°C). Line a muffin tin with paper liners.

step 2

In a mixing bowl, sift together the cake flour, baking powder, and salt.

step 3

In a separate bowl, beat the egg whites and cream of tartar with an electric mixer until stiff peaks form. Set aside.

step 4

In another mixing bowl, beat the egg yolks and sugar together until the mixture is light and fluffy.

step 5

Add the milk, melted butter, lemon zest, and vanilla extract to the egg yolk mixture and stir until well combined.

step 6

Gently fold the dry ingredients into the egg yolk mixture, using a spatula to combine them until there are no lumps.

step 7

Add a spoonful of the egg white mixture to the batter and fold it in gently, using a spatula to combine it. Continue adding the egg white mixture to the batter in batches, folding gently until the batter is fully incorporated.

step 8

Scoop the batter into the muffin tin, filling each paper liner about 2/3 full.

step 9

Bake the mamon for 15-20 minutes, or until the tops are lightly golden and a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.

step 10

Remove the mamon from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes in the muffin tin.

step 11

Transfer the mamon to a wire rack to cool completely.

step 12

Serve the mamon as a tasty snack or dessert.

ingredients

1 1/2 cup cake flour

1 1/2 teaspoon baking powder

1/4 teaspoon salt

6 large eggs, separated

1/2 teaspoon cream of tartar

1 cup sugar

1/2 cup milk

1/2 cup melted butter

1 teaspoon lemon zest

1 teaspoon vanilla extract
