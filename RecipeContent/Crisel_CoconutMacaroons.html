COCONUT MACAROONS

Macaroons have blown up in the US and these coconut versions are the perfect Filipino dessert to try out. They’re made with shredded coconut, sugar, and egg whites and they’re then baked in the oven. They can be flavored with all sorts of different things, like vanilla extract, chocolate, or coffee. You can also make them into different shapes and sizes to change them up every time you make them. These are always a hit at parties and gatherings and they’re a great way to show off your baking skills. They’re also really easy to make so you’ll be able to whip them up in no time.

instructions

step 1

Preheat your oven to 325°F (160°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

step 2

In a large mixing bowl, stir together the sweetened shredded coconut, sweetened condensed milk, vanilla extract, and salt until well combined.

step 3

In a separate bowl, beat the egg whites with an electric mixer until stiff peaks form.

step 4

Gently fold the beaten egg whites into the coconut mixture, using a spatula to combine the ingredients until the egg whites are fully incorporated.

step 5

If desired, fold in the chocolate chips to the mixture.

step 6

Bake the macaroons for 18-20 minutes, or until they are lightly golden on the outside

step 7

Remove the macaroons from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes on the baking sheet.

step 8

Transfer the macaroons to a wire rack to cool completely.

step 9

Store the macaroons in an airtight container at room temperature for up to a week.

ingredients

2 2/3 cups Sweetened shredded coconut

1 can (14 ounces) Sweetened condensed milk

Vanilla extract - 2 teaspoons

1/4 teaspoon Salt

2 large Egg whites

1/2 cup Chocolate chips (optional)
