CHOCOLATE PUDDING

Chocolate pudding is a chocolate-flavored custard made by cooking milk or cream with thickening agents such as eggs, flour, and cornstarch. The silky texture of chocolate pudding is not as airy as chocolate mousse, but it’s simpler to make at home.

instructions

step 1

In a medium saucepan, whisk together the sugar, cornstarch, cocoa powder, and salt.

step 2

Add the milk to the saucepan and whisk until the dry ingredients are completely dissolved.

step 3

Cook the mixture over medium heat, whisking constantly, until it comes to a simmer and starts to thicken. This should take about 8-10 minutes.

step 4

Once the mixture has thickened, remove it from the heat.

step 5

In a small bowl, whisk the egg yolks and vanilla extract together until they are well combined.

step 6

Slowly add a spoonful of the hot chocolate mixture to the egg yolks, whisking constantly to prevent the eggs from scrambling.

step 7

Continue adding the hot chocolate mixture to the egg yolks, a little at a time, until the egg mixture is warmed.

step 8

Pour the warmed egg mixture back into the saucepan with the remaining chocolate mixture and whisk to combine.

step 9

Cook the mixture over medium heat for an additional 2-3 minutes, whisking constantly, until the pudding has thickened.

step 10

Remove the pan from the heat and let the pudding cool for a few minutes.

step 11

Pour the pudding into a bowl or individual serving cups and let it cool to room temperature.

step 12

Cover the pudding with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or until it is fully chilled and set.

step 13

Serve the chocolate pudding cold, topped with whipped cream, chocolate shavings, or other toppings of your choice.

ingredients

2 cups (whole or 2%) of milk

1/2 cup Sugar

1/4 cup Cornstarch

1/4 cup Cocoa powder

1/4 teaspoon salt

3 pieces large egg yolks

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Optional Toppings

Whipped cream

Chocolate chips or shavings

Crushed nuts or cookies

Fresh fruit

Nutrition

Calories 140-200 KCal
Fat 2-9g
Carbohydrates 26-34g
Fiber 1-3g
Sugars 15-24g
Protein 3-5g
